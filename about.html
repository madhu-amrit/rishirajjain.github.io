About

Over the years, new printing processes have made possible the printing of many unusual stamps - stamps printed on all sorts of foil, metal, material, fabric, 3-D stamps, holograms, lenticular (movement) stamps and others.

Unusual Stamps is a website intended for purchase of stamps with an unusual shape. Many countries release such stamps for commercial as well as collection purposes.
